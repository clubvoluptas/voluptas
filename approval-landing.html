A Personal Note
from Jenn

Jenn · Founder, Voluptas

I read through what you shared with us, and you feel like our people.

Not because of what you're curious about. Because of why.

The couples who belong here aren't looking for an escape from their marriage. They're looking for more of each other, in a room that makes that possible. They made a decision somewhere along the way that good wasn't enough. That the heat their marriage was capable of deserved to be honored, not quietly accepted as something that belonged to their twenties.

That's who fills this Society. Devoted. Deeply married. Hungry for something most couples never admit to wanting, let alone pursue together.

This call is simply us meeting. I'll walk you through how the evenings work, what the Society looks like between gatherings, and how couples move through the room entirely on their own terms. You ask everything. We decide honestly together whether this is the right fit.

Thirty minutes. Both of you, together.

Jenn
FOUNDER, VOLUPTAS
After We Speak

If we're aligned, a fifty dollar reservation holds your place and opens your first month inside the Inner Ring, where the Society lives between gatherings. Event dates, invitations, and member connection exist only inside. None of it is ever posted publicly.

Membership continues at fifty dollars a month. Only as long as it feels right.

If after we speak this isn't the fit, your reservation comes back to you in full. No friction. No awkward follow-up.

But if it is the fit, what follows will be something you talk about on the drive home, and again over coffee the next morning, and again six months from now when you're already planning the next one.
