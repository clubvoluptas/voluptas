The Private Letter

What follows is known only to members. The Invitation, the possibility, what awaits you... none of it exists outside this page. But before you read further, understand what you are actually choosing.

Not a weekend. Not an event. Not a night in a penthouse above Las Vegas.

You are choosing an erotically alive marriage.

The journey home where you’re already planning the next one. The way the week after feels different from every week before it. The particular aliveness that only exists between two people who keep choosing to go further together. This is what attending The Experience actually means.

Afterwards, couples find themselves weeks, sometimes months, into a high that belongs entirely to them. A new level of alive. The kind that makes ordinary Tuesdays feel charged, that turns a glance across a room into something that means more than it did the week before.

This invitation is not a party. It is a choice to take what you already have somewhere it has never been. What follows is how it unfolds.

Husbands: your morning is your own. Spend it missing her.

The penthouse has been reserved entirely for the Society. No outside guests. No strangers. Only the couples who belong in this room.

Completely.

A Few Things to Consider
Before Accepting Your Invitation.

The items below are the conditions of your invitation. Your agreement to them is required before payment is accepted.

01 · Private Gathering

The Voluptas Experience is a private, invitation-only social gathering of Club Voluptas members, not a ticketed event. The venue, schedule, and guest list are confidential and disclosed to registered members only.

02 · Married Couples Only

Club Voluptas is exclusively for married couples. Both partners must attend together. Membership may be revoked if this is found to be misrepresented at any time.

03 · Absolute Confidentiality

You will not identify, photograph, tag, or discuss any other member, in person, online, or anywhere else, without their explicit written consent. This obligation has no expiration date.

04 · No Recording. Ever.

Phones are collected at The Gathering. No photography or recording of any kind is permitted at any Voluptas experience, and the venue location may never be shared publicly. These rules are absolute.

05 · Consent Is the Architecture

Everything at Voluptas is built around choice. Nothing is expected. Nothing is required. Consent is ongoing and reversible at any time. Anyone who disregards another member's boundaries is permanently removed.

06 · Attire Matters

Appropriate attire is required for entry to The Gathering. A style reference is available in the member society. Entry may be denied for attire that does not meet the standard, for either partner.

07 · All Fees Are Non-Refundable

The invitation reflects creation, coordination, and access. If you need to cancel, fees are non-refundable. If Voluptas cancels, you choose between a full refund or credit. If force majeure intervenes, a 24-month credit is issued.

One More Thing Before You Pay

After payment is confirmed, you will receive a private Participant Agreement by email. Both spouses sign individually before your arrival window is released. It is the agreement that protects the atmosphere of discretion and the trust the Society is built on.
